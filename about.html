The University Chess Club was founded to bring together students passionate about chess. Our mission is to promote critical thinking, concentration, and camaraderie through friendly matches, tournaments, and coaching sessions.

We welcome players of all skill levels — from beginners to advanced competitors.

Chess Team

We believe that every move counts-on the board as a team.We play not to just win,but to learn grow ,and support each other.Every victory is shared,every mistake is a lesson,and every game makes us stronger together.Our spirit is built on respect,discipline and the joy of chess.Win or lose,we move foward as one team ,one family
